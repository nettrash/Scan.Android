Version 1.1

• Recognises more barcode payloads: GS1 product codes (parens, FNC1 and Digital Link forms), IATA boarding passes (PDF417), and AAMVA identity-document barcodes.
• Magnet links, vCard 4.0 and rich URLs (WhatsApp, Telegram, YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, App Store, Google Play) now get tailored smart actions.
• Extra cryptocurrency chains — XRP, Stellar, Cosmos and Lightning addresses.
• Stability fixes and parser improvements.
