Scan reads and generates the common 1D and 2D barcodes — and understands what's actually inside the code, not just the raw text.

Point the camera at a Wi-Fi QR and you'll see the network name, security type, and a one-tap shortcut to Wi-Fi Settings. Scan a SEPA invoice and you get the IBAN, beneficiary, and amount as separate copyable rows. Scan a contact card and the system "New Contact" form opens pre-filled. Scan a calendar event and the "Add Event" sheet appears with the title, location, and times in your time zone.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Scan does not collect, transmit, or share any data. The barcode model runs entirely on-device — camera frames are analysed in memory and never written to disk or sent over the network. The history is stored locally in the app's private database. No accounts, no telemetry, no advertising SDKs, no trackers.

SMART RESULT SHEET

Scan understands these payload formats and surfaces each field as a tap-to-copy row:

• Web — URL, mailto, tel, sms / smsto
• Wi-Fi — SSID, password, security, hidden flag
• Location — geo: links opened in Maps
• Contact — vCard 3.0 and MECARD
• Calendar — iCalendar VEVENT (UTC, time-zone, all-day)
• Authentication — otpauth:// for authenticator apps
• Retail — EAN / UPC / ITF product codes
• Cryptocurrency — Bitcoin (BIP-21), Ethereum (EIP-681 with chain ID), Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash, Dogecoin, Monero, Cardano, Solana, Lightning (BOLT-11)
• Bank payments — EPC SEPA Payment QR / GiroCode (EU), Swiss QR-bill, Czech SPD, Slovak Pay by Square, Russian unified payment (ST00012 / ST00011), EMVCo Merchant QR with drill-down for Pix, PayNow, PromptPay, CoDi, UPI-via-EMVCo, DuitNow, QRIS, FPS, NAPAS, NETS, Indian UPI, Bezahlcode, Serbian NBS IPS QR (Prenesi)
• Mobile-payment apps — Swish, Vipps, MobilePay, Bizum, iDEAL
• Receipts — Russian FNS retail receipt, Serbian SUF fiscal receipt
